# 🚗 Car Sales Performance Dashboard  

## 📌 End-to-End Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Project  

A comprehensive data analytics project focused on analyzing car sales performance across revenue, customers, dealers, and product preferences. This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights to support strategic decision-making in automotive sales and market positioning.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement  

Automotive businesses often struggle to answer:

- Which car types generate the highest revenue?  
- Who are the top-performing dealers and customers?  
- How does pricing impact revenue generation?  
- What are customer preferences across different segments?  
- How does sales performance change over time?  

Raw transactional data alone cannot clearly reveal these insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective  

To design an interactive dashboard that helps:

- Track overall revenue and sales performance  
- Analyze customer and dealer contribution  
- Understand body style preferences  
- Evaluate pricing vs revenue relationship  
- Identify trends in sales over time  

---

## 📊 Executive Summary  

- 💰 672M Total Revenue Generated  
- 🚗 24K Cars Sold  
- 🏢 30 Companies Analyzed  
- 🚘 154 Models Evaluated  
- 🤝 28 Dealers Performance Tracked  
- 📈 Time-based revenue trend analysis

# 📈 Key Business Insights  

## 🏆 Revenue Performance Overview  

- Strong overall revenue with consistent growth trend  

**Insight:**  
Sales performance shows upward momentum with periodic fluctuations.

---

## 👨‍💼 Dealer Performance  

- Top dealers contribute significantly to total revenue  

**Insight:**  
Revenue is concentrated among a few high-performing dealers.

---

## 👥 Customer Contribution  

- Top customers generate a noticeable share of revenue  

**Insight:**  
Customer concentration indicates potential dependency risk.

---

## 🚗 Body Style Preference  

- SUVs and Hatchbacks generate the highest revenue share  

**Insight:**  
Customer preference is skewed toward specific car types.

---

## 📊 Pricing vs Revenue Relationship  

- Higher-priced vehicles generally contribute more revenue  
- However, mid-range pricing shows strong volume-driven performance  

**Insight:**  
Balanced pricing strategy is key for maximizing revenue.

---

## 👩‍🦱 Gender-Based Insights  

- Male customers contribute a larger share of total revenue  
- Preferences vary across body styles  

**Insight:**  
Customer segmentation can improve targeted marketing.

---

## 📅 Sales Trend Analysis  

- Revenue fluctuates across months with noticeable peaks  

**Insight:**  
Seasonality plays an important role in sales performance.

---

## 📊 Revenue Concentration  

- Revenue is concentrated among top dealers, customers, and car types  

**Insight:**  
Business performance is not evenly distributed.

---

## 💼 Strategic Recommendations  

- Reduce dependency on top dealers and customers  
- Focus on high-demand car segments (SUVs, Hatchbacks)  
- Optimize pricing strategy for better volume + margin balance  
- Use customer segmentation for targeted campaigns  
- Plan sales strategies around seasonal trends
